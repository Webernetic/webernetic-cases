What was done?

1

We analyzed the competitors' actions and updated the structure of the site to meet modern standards and the needs of our audience.

2

We updated the design of the site, made it more modern and consistent with the branding of the company. We worked on the product catalog, product page and news section.

Ubuntu

Result

We completely redesigned the client's existing website, focusing on easy navigation and presentation of the company's products. The whole process was carried out by our team, from analysis to testing. The customer was completely satisfied with the result of our work. We worked in close cooperation at all stages of the process, taking into account all the wishes and requirements.

The result is a website that reflects the company's corporate style and demonstrates a wide range of products. This will allow the company to increase its recognition and attract new customers.
